Project Overview

Experience the future of energy storage with our graphene cement supercapacitors, enabling lightning-fast wireless charging for electric vehicles.

Wireless Charging Innovation

This demonstration showcases our revolutionary graphene cement supercapacitors enabling rapid wireless charging of an RC car. The system features:

  • Qi-compatible wireless charging pad
  • Instant energy transfer to supercapacitors
  • Real-time charging status indicators
  • Public phone charging station

The RC car automatically moves to the charging station when energy is low, demonstrating our supercapacitors' ability to charge in seconds rather than minutes.
